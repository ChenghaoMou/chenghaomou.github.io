Multidimensional Exponential Moving Average (MEMA)

This is a multidimensional extension of Exponential Moving Average (EMA).

Define the mini-batch input sequence X∈RB×S×d×1, an expansion matrix β∈Rd×h, the expanded representation is calculated as:
